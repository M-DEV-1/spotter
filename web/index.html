How It Works
1. Sim
Franka Panda arm in MuJoCo. Classical keyframe actor. EGL headless on DGX Spark (ARM64).
2. Failure signals
Cheap ground-truth: gripper_closed_empty, cube_out_of_region, no_progress. No pixel guessing.
3. Gemma supervisor
Camera frame + signals → Gemma 4 31B → structured correction: failure_type + diagnosis + instruction.
4. Recovery
Actor retries with corrected joint aim (atan2 offset). Gemma is the coach, not the planner.
